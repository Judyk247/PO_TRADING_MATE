🎯 Strategy Overview

Trend Following (1m, 2m, 3m): Follows established trend with Alligator alignment, EMA slope confirmation, and Stochastic in 20-40/60-80 zones.

⚙️ Martingale System

When enabled, after a loss the bot will place a new trade at 2.3x the previous amount, continuing until a win is achieved (unlimited steps).
